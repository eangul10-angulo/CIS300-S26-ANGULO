Baking Tips

Baking gets a lot easier when you start with a few simple habits that make every recipe more predictable. Using room-temperature ingredients helps everything mix more smoothly and creates a more even texture. Before you start baking, read the entire recipe so you don't get caught off guard by chill times or missing ingredients. And always make sure your oven is fully preheated, consistent heat is key for proper rising and baking, especially since many ovens run a little hotter or cooler than the dial suggests.

Tools

Keeping a few reliable tools on hand makes baking so much easier. A digital scale helps you measure ingredients accurately, good-quality pans ensure even baking, and a reliable oven thermometer lets you know your oven's true temperature so your treats bake exactly the way they should.
