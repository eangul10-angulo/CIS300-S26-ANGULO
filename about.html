About Me

I'm Emily, a home baker who fell in love with mixing batter and decorating cakes back when I was a teen. I love experimenting with flavors, learning new pastry techniques, and surprising friends and family with homemade treats, especially birthday cakes for the people I love most. Baking is my favorite way to unwind, and this site is where I collect and share the recipes that make me happiest.

My Baking Background

I started baking by making tiny birthday cakes for my dogs, then moved on to cupcakes for my friends on my cheer team. Before long, I was collecting baking books and experimenting with new recipes whenever I had free time. Nothing makes me happier than seeing someone's face light up when they taste one of my treats. To share that joy more widely, I began writing down my recipes and breaking them into clear, simple steps so anyone can bake along with me.
